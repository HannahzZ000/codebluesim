What We Built

Four training modes, one platform

CodeBlueSim offers curricula covering Basic Life Support (BLS), Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS), leadership, and teamwork — supporting both out-of-hospital and in-hospital scenarios.

XR Experience

Immersive, hands-on training

Using hand tracking and spatial computing, trainees interact directly with medical equipment in a realistic 3D environment. SharePlay enables team members' virtual avatars to appear together in the same space.

Research

AWE 2026 Research Poster

Co-designed with clinical experts, spatial designers, and developers. Based on AHA ACLS guidelines, peer-reviewed resuscitation literature, simulation-based education principles, and human factors engineering.
